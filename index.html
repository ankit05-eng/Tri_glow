Our Story

The Brothers Behind Triglow

Three brothers from Odisha turned a dream into a thriving mushroom farm.

Triglow was born in Pokatunga, Bantala, Angul, Odisha — where three brothers decided to grow mushrooms and bring freshness to every table. Starting with one shed and pure determination, they built a farm that now serves hundreds of happy customers.

The name "Triglow" represents the three glowing brothers — each bringing their unique skills to make the farm a success. From soil to your plate, every mushroom is grown with care.
