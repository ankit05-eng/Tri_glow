Health & Nutrition

Why Mushrooms?

Packed with nutrients, mushrooms are a superfood that benefits your body in multiple ways.

High in Protein

Mushrooms contain all essential amino acids making them an excellent plant-based protein source for vegetarians.

Heart Healthy

Rich in potassium and fibre, mushrooms help lower blood pressure and maintain healthy cholesterol levels.

Boosts Immunity

Beta-glucans and antioxidants in mushrooms strengthen the immune system and fight inflammation naturally.

Weight Management

Low in calories and high in fibre, mushrooms keep you full longer and support healthy weight management.

Vitamin D Source

Mushrooms naturally produce Vitamin D when exposed to sunlight — great for bone health and mood.

Organic Farming

We grow without pesticides or chemicals. Our substrate-based cultivation ensures the purest mushrooms you can eat.

Our Story

Meet the Brothers

Three brothers from Odisha with one dream — to bring the freshest mushrooms to every home.

Triglow was born in the small village of Pokatunga, Bantala, Angul, Odisha — where three brothers decided to turn their passion for agriculture into a thriving mushroom farming business.

Starting with a single cultivation shed and a bag of substrate, they built Triglow from the ground up — learning, failing, growing, and eventually succeeding. Today, Triglow is one of the region's trusted names for fresh, organic mushrooms.

The name "Triglow" stands for the three glowing brothers who light up the mushroom farming world with their dedication, hard work, and love for nature.
