Meet the Team

The Brothers of Triglow

Three brothers from Pokatunga, Angul, Odisha who turned a dream into a thriving mushroom farm.

Triglow was born in Pokatunga, Bantala, Angul, Odisha — where three brothers decided to grow fresh mushrooms and serve every home with purity and nutrition. Starting with one shed and zero experience, they learned, worked hard, and built a farm that now feeds hundreds of happy families across Odisha.

The name "Triglow" stands for the three glowing brothers — Amulya, Ankit and Amit — each bringing a unique strength to build something beautiful together from the heart of Odisha.

Business Manager

Amulya is the backbone of Triglow. He handles all business operations, financial planning, supplier relationships and daily management. His steady leadership keeps the farm profitable and growing.
